# Meeting schedule

We have a monthly online meeting every 2nd Thursday of the month at 15:00 (Berlin time).

We meet online either on:

- [WorkAdventure](https://play.workadventu.re/@/nf-core/nf-core/office) (suitable for up to 30 people)
- or Zoom, as an alternative

The platform is normally decided by vote on the day prior to the meeting on the nf-core Slack.

To get the joining links, or to be added to a Google Calendar event, please ask on the [#meta-omics](https://nfcore.slack.com/archives/C070369GP7T) channel on the nf-core Slack.

Past meeting notes are available on [HackMD](https://hackmd.io/@nf-core/SyCCVMkT0).
