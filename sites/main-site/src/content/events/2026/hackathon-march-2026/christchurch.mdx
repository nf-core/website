Any bioinformatics enthusiasts in Christchurch are welcome to attend this local node of the nf-core hackathon!

Attendees from outside Christchurch **can** attend.

Contacts:

- [Jen Reeve](https://nfcore.slack.com/team/U09NYJP8WJ1) (jennifer.reeve@reannz.co.nz)
- [Chris Hakkaart](https://nfcore.slack.com/team/U024MM1DRKL) (chris.hakkaart@seqera.io)

## Site details

The Christchurch site will be held somewhere in Christchurch, New Zealand. The exact location is still to be determined, but it will be an in-person event.

## Schedule

This event will be held on the 11th of March, 2026, from 10:00 to 14:00 local time (NZDT).

We will keep the schedule flexible to allow for collaboration and discussion among attendees.

## Projects

We will be working on updating the [Genomics Aotearoa Nextflow Workshop](https://github.com/GenomicsAotearoa/Nextflow_Workshop).

Key focus will be on updating the workshop to use the latest version of Nextflow and nf-core pipelines, as well as adding new content and exercises.
