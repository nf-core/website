We are hosting a local hub for the nf-core hackathon for the first time in Okinawa. We plan to use this event as a way to introduce Nextflow and nf-core to the local bioinformatics community in Okinawa so feel free to join even if you don't have any experience with Nextflow. We can host up to 12 people.

Main contacts:
Lea Picard ([LEA.PICARD@OIST.JP](mailto:LEA.PICARD@OIST.JP))
Patricia Muskus ([PATRICIA.MUSKUS@OIST.JP](mailto:PATRICIA.MUSKUS@OIST.JP))

## Projects

We will be working on the developement of [nf-core/genephylo](https://github.com/nf-core/genephylo), including with the creation of nf-core modules for software implemented in the pipeline.

Any other people wanting to lead a particular pipeline at this event should register their project on the hackathon page, and let us know.
