## Hackathon at Helmholtz Munich - Core Facility Bioinformatics and Statistics

We are hosting a hackathon hub at Helmholtz Munich - Core Facility Bioinformatics and Statistics. If you are interested in working together on our project and want to join us in person, please get in touch with the main contact below. Please note that we have a limited number of seats available, so we encourage you to register early to secure your spot.

## Venue Details

We welcome attendees from outside Helmholtz Munich to join us. A valid ID card or passport is required to access the campus.

### Parking

There are parking spaces available next to the building.

### Food

We have a cafeteria for lunch, snacks, and coffee that accepts cards.

## Project

We aim to extend the [nf-core/scrnaseq](https://nf-co.re/scrnaseq/dev/) pipeline with functionality for RNA velocity calculation, sample aggregation, and alternative chemistries. For further information about the project and the tasks go to the project [nf-core/scrnaseq](https://nf-co.re/hackathon-projects/hackathon-march-2026/hgmu_projects).
