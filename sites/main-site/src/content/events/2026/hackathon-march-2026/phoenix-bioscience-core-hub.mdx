Welcome to the **Phoenix Bioscience Core** in-person local hub for the global nf-core Hackathon March 11–13, 2026!

Join fellow bioinformaticians, nf-core contributors, students, postdocs, PIs, and industry professionals in downtown Phoenix to collaboratively build and improve nf-core pipelines, modules, tools, and documentation.

This local hub is proudly hosted by the **University of Arizona** in partnership with Arizona's leading research and biotech ecosystem (including ASU, NAU, TGen, major health systems, and emerging companies).

## Schedule & Logistics

- **Local hours**: Approximately 9:00 AM – 5:00 PM MST each day (flexible for breaks, meals, and networking)
- **Capacity**: 50–60 participants
- **Format**: In-person only at this hub
- **Experience level**: All welcome! No prior nf-core or Nextflow experience required — beginners to experts encouraged.

We'll follow the main hackathon schedule while focusing on approved nf-core projects.
