## Projects

We will work on open issues around nf-core and health genomics relevant to Ghana and Africa, including:

- **nf-core/modules:** Contributing to module updates, testing, and migration to the latest Nextflow strict syntax with no errors or warnings.
- **nf-core/phaseimpute:** Supporting development and testing of this pipeline, suitable for contributors at all skill levels.
- **Pathogen genomics workflows:** Adapting nf-core pipelines for malaria, viral, and antimicrobial resistance genomics applications relevant to NMIMR research programs.

If you and your colleagues are interested in collaborating on a project at the hackathon, feel free to submit a pull request (PR) to include your project. We welcome Ghana- and Africa-focused bioinformatics initiatives.

## About NMIMR

The Noguchi Memorial Institute for Medical Research (NMIMR), established in 1979 as a semi-autonomous institute of the University of Ghana, is the leading biomedical research facility in Ghana.  

### Core Mandate

- Conduct innovative research into communicable and non-communicable diseases  
- Provide high-end laboratory diagnostics, monitoring, and surveillance services in support of global health  
- Provide capacity building in health services  

### Main Research Areas

- Malaria  
- Bacterial infections  
- Neglected tropical diseases (NTDs)  
- Antimicrobial resistance  
- Viral infections (including COVID-19 and HIV)  
- Nutrition
