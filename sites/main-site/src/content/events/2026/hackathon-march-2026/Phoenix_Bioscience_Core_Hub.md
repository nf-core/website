# nf-core Bioinformatics Hackathon – March 2026  
**Phoenix Bioscience Core Local Hub**

Welcome to the **Phoenix Bioscience Core** in-person hub for the global nf-core Hackathon!

Join fellow bioinformaticians, nf-core contributors, students, postdocs, PIs, and industry professionals in downtown Phoenix to collaboratively build and improve nf-core pipelines, modules, tools, and documentation.

This local hub is proudly hosted by the University of Arizona.

- **Dates**: March 11–13, 2026 (Wednesday–Friday)
- **Format**: In-person only at this hub (synchronized with the main hybrid nf-core event)
