Attendees from outside the University of Pavia **can** attend.

Main contact: Francesco Lescai ([francesco.lescai@unipv.it](mailto:francesco.lescai@unipv.it))

## How to find us

Information to reach the venue are available [here](https://matematica.unipv.it/en/how-to-reach-us/). We will send contact information to the attendees prior to the meeeting and we will be guiding guests to the Hackathon Room.

## Organisational Information

There is a cafeteria close by - [La Bouvette dell'Ingegnere](https://www.instagram.com/labouvetteunipv/), which accepts both card and cash.
Water, coffee and tea will be provided.

We will arrange a social dinner in one of the evenings if there is interest among the attendees.

## Accommodation

We assume most participants will join from within driving / local train range:
should you need suggestions or help arranging an accommodation, just get in touch with
[Francesco](https://nfcore.slack.com/team/UT486V1V1) or [Mariangela](https://nfcore.slack.com/team/U04501R6BLL).
