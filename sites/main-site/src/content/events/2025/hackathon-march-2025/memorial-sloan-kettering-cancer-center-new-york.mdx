## Training Session March 20th, 2025

Ahead of the hackathon, we'll have a warmup training session for `nextflow`/`nf-core` enthusiasts to get started or advance their knowledge.

We'll have talks from:

- Jeff Quinn, from the Tansley Lab.
- Juan Arango Ossa, from the Papaemmanuil Lab.
- Simran Chhabria, from the Morris Lab.
- Eric Buelher and Anne Marie Noronha, from the Berger Lab.

Ranging topics about fundamentals, best practices, nextflow vs others, nf-core institutional configs, pipeline and hackathon ideas.
