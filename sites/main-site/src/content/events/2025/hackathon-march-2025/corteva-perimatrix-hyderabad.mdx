## Site specific guidelines

The hackathon site is located at Ascendas IT Park, 8th floor, Atria, Madhapur, Telangana, India.
External participants are accepted and require pre-approved access.
The participants are requested to register at least a week before the event.

The first day of the event includes talks on topics introducing Nextflow nf-core resource, by Sateesh Peri (Perimatrix IT),
Nextflow on K8s by Vijay Ambasta (Corteva Agriscience) & Nextflow on Cloud by Vijay Narsapuram (Corteva Agriscience).

The maximum capacity of the site is 25 pax and the registration will be accepted on 'first come first serve' basis.
