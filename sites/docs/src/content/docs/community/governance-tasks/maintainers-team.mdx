This page provides step-by-step procedures for maintainers team members to perform common tasks, including onboarding new members, managing team membership, and hosting helpdesk sessions.

## Onboard maintainers team

To add maintainers:

1. Team lead:
    1. Add member to [#team-maintainers](https://nfcore.slack.com/archives/C043UU89KKQ).
    1. Add member to [Github group](https://github.com/orgs/nf-core/teams/maintainers).
    1. Add member to Slack group.
    1. Add member to the [Maintainers team google calendar](https://calendar.google.com/calendar/u/0?cid=ZDA3MmVkOGI1NzQ0YWViOWE3ZjBkMDE4YjFjZWE1OGE5M2MwZDZlZTIyMzM1YWE5YzA0NTlmODg0YTZkMDNkZEBncm91cC5jYWxlbmRhci5nb29nbGUuY29t).
1. New member:
    1. Add profile to [**About > Governance > Maintainers**](https://nf-co.re/governance#maintainers).

## Review maintainers team membership

:::note
New maintainers may be nominated by the community and join the team at any time.
Maintainers maintenance is performed yearly to ensure membership is up-to-date, particularly in regards to members who wish to step down due to new or changing commitments.
:::

To review mainterins team membership:

1. Ask who wants to step down.
1. Ask for nominations, discuss, and approve new members.
1. Invite nominees to join.
1. Once accepted, nominee and team leads perform tasks [onboarding](#maintainers-team-onboarding).
1. Move members to alumni status on the website in [**About > Governance > Maintainers**](https://nf-co.re/governance#maintainers).
1. Announce new members on [#announcements](https://nfcore.slack.com/archives/CE6P95170).

## Host helpdesk

To host a helpdesk:

1. Monthly basis:
    - Check to see for open slots, and make sure sign up on weekly signup sheet.
1. When hosting (on the day):
    - 1 hour before, advertise on social media (core) and on `#weekly-helpdesk`.
    - Sit on community platform or huddle for one hour.
    - Afterwards dump detailed notes in the Hackamd.
    - Post a short summary on `#weekly-helpdesk`.
    - Send a reminder to next weeks hosts.

:::note
Hosting a helpdesk is not exclusive to maintainers and we encourage community members interested to get involved.
:::
